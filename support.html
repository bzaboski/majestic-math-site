Frequently Asked Questions

What ages is Majestic Math designed for?

Majestic Math is designed for children ages 4–12, with a primary focus on ages 4–8. The adaptive difficulty system adjusts to each child's level, so younger and older children can both use the app effectively.

Can I create profiles for multiple children?

Yes! One parent account can manage multiple child profiles. Each child gets their own persona, avatar, progress, currency, and learning path. One purchase covers the whole family.

Does the app work offline?

Yes. All lessons, practice questions, and tests work without an internet connection. Progress syncs to the cloud automatically when a connection is available.

How does the placement assessment work?

When a child starts for the first time, they take a short adaptive assessment (about 10 questions). Based on their performance, the app places them in the right tier so they start at the right level — not too easy, not too hard.

What math topics are covered?

Majestic Math covers K–5 math fundamentals: counting (1-10, 1-20, skip counting by 2s, 5s, and 10s), addition (single and double digit), subtraction, multiplication (times tables 1-5), and division (equal sharing). More topics are coming in future updates.

Is my child's data safe?

Absolutely. Majestic Math is COPPA compliant. We don't collect personal information from children without parental consent, we don't use advertising SDKs, we don't track children, and we never sell data to anyone. Read our full Privacy Policy for details.

How do I delete my child's data?

You can delete any child profile and all associated data from the parent dashboard in the app. To delete your entire account (parent and all children), email us at support@zteamapps.com.

What are the coins, silver, and gold for?

These are in-app currencies earned through practice and tests. Children spend them in the Avatar Shop to customize their Knight or Princess character with outfits, accessories, and more. All currency is earned through learning — there are no in-app purchases.

Who built Majestic Math?

Majestic Math was built by the Z-Team — a dad and his three daughters (ages 4, 6, and 8). Every feature was tested by real kids, and the app was designed to make math feel like a magical adventure, not homework.
