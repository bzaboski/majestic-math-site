Majestic Math — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

Majestic Math ("the App") is developed and published by Z-Team Apps ("we", "us", or "our"). We take your family's privacy seriously — especially the privacy of children. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a parent or guardian.

Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Majestic Math is designed for children ages 4–12 and complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not collect personal information from children without verifiable parental consent.

  • Child profiles are created and managed exclusively by a parent or guardian.
  • No data collection or cloud synchronization occurs until a parent has completed account creation and the parental consent gate.
  • We do not collect device identifiers, location data, or behavioral analytics from child users.
  • There is no user-to-user communication or social features in the App.
  • There are no third-party advertising SDKs in the App.
  • There are no third-party analytics SDKs that track children.

Information We Collect

Parent Account Information

When a parent creates an account, we collect:

  • Email address (for authentication and account recovery)
  • Password (stored securely using industry-standard hashing — we never see your plain-text password)

If you use Apple Sign-In, we receive only the information you choose to share (which may include your email or a private relay address).

Child Profile Information

Parents create child profiles that include:

  • First name or nickname (chosen by the parent)
  • Grade level (used to calibrate lesson difficulty)
  • Selected persona (Knight or Princess)
  • Avatar customization choices (hair, eyes, skin tone)
  • Optional PIN for child login

Learning Data

The App collects learning progress data to provide an adaptive educational experience:

  • Lesson completion status
  • Practice and test session scores
  • Skill mastery levels
  • Placement assessment results
  • In-app currency balances and shop inventory

This data is used solely to personalize your child's learning experience and to display progress on the parent dashboard.

How We Use Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Provide and personalize the educational experience
  • Adapt question difficulty to your child's level
  • Track learning progress and display it on the parent dashboard
  • Sync data across devices (when online)
  • Authenticate parent accounts

We do not use any collected information for advertising, marketing to children, or sale to third parties.

Data Storage and Security

Data is stored using Supabase, a secure cloud platform with PostgreSQL database and row-level security (RLS). All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption. Parent accounts can only access their own children's data.

Learning data is also cached locally on the device to enable offline use.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share any personal information or children's data with third parties. Period.

The only third-party service that receives data is our cloud database provider (Supabase), which acts as a data processor on our behalf and is subject to their own privacy and security obligations.

Parental Rights

As a parent or guardian, you have the right to:

  • Review your child's personal information and learning data via the parent dashboard.
  • Delete your child's profile and all associated data at any time from the parent settings.
  • Delete your account and all associated data (parent and all child profiles) by contacting us.
  • Withdraw consent for data collection at any time by deleting child profiles or your account.

Offline Use

Majestic Math works offline. When offline, data is stored locally on the device and synchronized to the cloud when an internet connection is restored. No data is collected or transmitted while offline.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
